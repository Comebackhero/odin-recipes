A Beginner's Guide to Ramen Recipes

This Japanese noodle soup is conquering the world, one umami-bomb bowl at a time. From broth to noodles to toppings, ramen varies based on region and specialties. It is near impossible to scratch the surface of what a delicious bowl of ramen is, but nonetheless, here is a start to get you drooling and dreaming of your next bowl of ramen!

What is Ramen?

Ramen is a Japanese adaptation of the Chinese Lamian. To call it "ramen," it must consist of wheat noodles and soup. While there are several contested theories on how and when it orginially arrived in Japan, ramen has always been cheap and filling, the food of champions for blue-collar workers. There are hundreds of varieties of ramen across the world

Making ramen at home is truly a labor of love. This isn't some 15 minute miracle insta-ramen recipe. Making authentic ramen takes time. It takes effort. You have to be a bit crazy to go there, but it's so good. I promise.
