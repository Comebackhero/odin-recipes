Spicy Ramen Recipe

This Spicy Ramen recipe is made in just about 15 minutes with a soy-ginger broth that's perfectly spicy and savory. Perfect topped with soft boiled eggs, green onions, nori, or chicken, pork or tofu, it's an excellent and budget friendly lunch!

Prep: 2 minutes

Cook: 13 minutes

Servings: 2

Calories: 194 kcal

Ingredients

  • 4 cups broth (like chicken or vegetable broth)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 1" knob ginger (cut into 4 slices)
  • 2 cloves garlic (peeled and smashed)
  • 1 TBSP miso paste
  • 1 TBSP sambal oelek
  • 2 TBSP soy sauce
  • 2 TSP sesame oil
  • Dash of rice wine vinegar
  • 2 blocks of ramen or servings fresh ramen

Toppings

  • 2 soft-boiled eggs
  • Scallions
  • Black sesame seeds
  • 1/2 piece nori
  • Shredded or chopped chicken or pork or cubed tofu

Instructions

  1. Cook your eggs now or make them ahead of time and refridgerate until ready to use.
  2. Combine broth, ginger, garlic, miso, sambal oelek, soy sauce, sesame oil, and vinegar in a saucepan. Bring everything to a boil.
  3. Reduce ehat to let simmer and cook 10 minutes.
  4. Spoon out the ginger and garlic slices.
  5. Add dried noodles and cook until soft. Divide noodles evenly between two bowls.
  6. Cover with broth and top with eggs, scallions, black sesame seeds, and nori.
  7. Enjoy!
